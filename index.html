1. Community Overview

About JVC

Jumeirah Village Circle (JVC) is one of Dubai's most popular and rapidly expanding freehold communities. It features a diverse mix of residential units, from affordable apartments to luxury villas, making it a prime destination for both renters and investors due to its affordability and central location.

Developer: Nakheel

Size: Approx. 92M SQFT (Significantly Larger than JVT)

Vibe: Vibrant, High Density, Investment Hub

2. Connectivity & Community Features

Major Roads and Connectivity

JVC's distinct circular layout provides multiple access points, ensuring smooth transit to all parts of Dubai, making it a highly strategic residential location.

  • Sheikh Mohammed Bin Zayed Road (E311): Excellent outward connections.
  • Al Khail Road (E44): Direct route to Dubai Marina and Downtown.
  • Hessa St (D61)
  • Location: Centrally positioned near Al Barsha and Dubai Sports City.
